Projects / Contributions

Cleansweep
A volunteer management system for Aam Adami Party.

Started by veteran programmer Anand Chitipothu, Cleansweep tracks and manages the activities of party volunteers across all polling booths, polling stations, wards, assembly constituencies, districts and states.

I've been an active developer of cleansweep since Sep '15. I helped scrape GBs of electoral data, build APIs, SMS Bridge and many more things.
